## What this demo shows

- `CustomEffect` extending `CenterBurstEffect`.
- Delayed transition from shatter to recall phase.
- Active shard orchestration with attraction and selective shard removal.

## Key APIs

- `CustomEffect`
- `attractShards(ownerId, targetPx, impulsePx, maxDistancePx)`
- `removeShard(shardId)`
- `bodySnapshot(id)` and `shardSnapshots(ownerId)`
