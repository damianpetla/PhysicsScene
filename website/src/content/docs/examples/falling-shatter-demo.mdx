## What this demo shows

- Two buttons activated with `activateBody(id)`.
- Different shard collider shapes (`Box` and `Circle`).
- Lifecycle transitions and respawn flow.

## Key APIs

- `FallingShatterEffect`
- `activateBody(id)`
- `respawnBody(id)`
- `resetScene()`

## Minimal interaction loop

1. Activate body.
2. Wait for shatter and removal events.
3. Respawn selected body or reset entire scene.
